INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

Are you tired of the repetitive process of updating your stock levels into all your different online stores?

With Ecomcj, you only need to upload a stock item once and it will reflect in each and every individual online store. You simply log into your account with Ecomcj and view all your stores in one place and edit your inventory.

When a sale is made or the inventory is updated, Ecomcj will automatically synchronise all the stock levels through all the shopping channels and show a common inventory level centrally as well as on an individual shop. This helps prevent overselling or underselling.

The Ecomcj software has proved to be very beneficial to traders who are selling through various retail channels such as Flipkart , Amazon, e.t.c, and find the management of the same difficult to keep on top of.

Ecomcj aims to simplify the management of multichannel ecommerce.
